About Erica

Warm support. Clear systems. A polished reset for your home.

Organizing is not just about making a space look good. It is about creating a home that supports your daily life. Erica helps clients thoughtfully sort through what they own, let go of what no longer serves them, and build systems that are simple to maintain.

Every home is different. That is why each project is approached with compassion, practical problem-solving, and an edited plan shaped around your routines, your family, and the way you truly use your space.

FAQ

Helpful details for first-time clients

Do I need to be fully ready before we start?

No. The process is designed to help you move from overwhelmed to clear, one step at a time.

Will you make me get rid of everything?

No. Decisions stay in your hands. The goal is thoughtful editing and practical function, not pressure.

Can you help with just one area of the home?

Yes. Many clients begin with a single closet, pantry, bedroom, or drop zone before expanding to other spaces.

What happens after the session?

You leave with clearer systems, easier routines, and a setup that helps every item return to a defined home.
